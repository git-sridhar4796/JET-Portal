JIBA Entrance Test (JET) 2026

Important Instructions

Please read all instructions carefully. You must agree to these terms before the system will activate your camera and allow you to begin the test.

Test Overview

  • •
    Duration: 30 minutes
  • •
    Questions: 30 multiple choice questions (MCQs)
  • •
    Marking: Each question carries 1 mark. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers.
  • •
    Question Types: The test contains questions from general aptitude, logical reasoning, quantitative ability, and english language skills.

Proctoring & Security

  • •
    Live Camera Monitoring: Your webcam will remain active throughout the test. Proctors will live-monitor the feed to ensure exam integrity. Any suspicious activity, such as looking away from the screen frequently or having unauthorized materials in view, may be flagged for review.
  • •
    Full-Screen Mode: Once the test begins, the browser will enter full-screen mode. Any attempt to exit full-screen will be logged as a violation.
  • •
    Tab-Switching: Switching tabs or minimizing the browser window is strictly prohibited and will be logged as a violation.
  • •
    Right-Click & Shortcuts: Right-clicking and keyboard shortcuts (like Ctrl+C, Ctrl+Shift+I, F12 or F5) are disabled to maintain test integrity.
  • •
    Warning System: You will receive a maximum of 2 warnings for leaving the test screen. On the 3rd instance, your exam will be automatically submitted immediately.

Navigation & Submission

  • •
    Reviewing Answers: You can navigate through all questions and change your selected options at any time before final submission.
  • •
    Progress Tracking: Use the "Stats" panel on the right to track your attempted and remaining questions as you proceed through the exam.
  • •
    Final Submission: Once you have answered all questions, click the "Submit Test" button. You will be asked for a final confirmation before the test ends.

Technical Requirements

  • •
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection with a minimum speed of 2-3 Mbps.
  • •
    Ensure your device is connected to a power source or fully charged. A sudden shutdown due to low battery may result in an incomplete submission.
  • •
    Do not click the browser’s "Back" or "Refresh" buttons, as this may result in a loss of data.
  • •
    Close all background applications, especially communication tools (WhatsApp, Discord, Zoom) and screen-sharing software, to prevent performance lag or focus-loss violations.
  • •
    If you face any technical issues, do not close the portal. Contact the Technical Support Team immediately via the contact number provided in the "Stats" Panel.
